# Day 74: DevOps Culture & Team Practices

## 🎯 What You'll Learn

- DevOps culture principles
- Team collaboration patterns
- Organizational transformation
- Measuring culture success

## 🤝 DevOps Culture Principles

- **Shared Responsibility:** Dev and Ops collaborate
- **Fail Fast, Learn Fast:** Embrace failure as learning
- **Continuous Improvement:** Kaizen mindset

## 👥 Team Collaboration

- Cross-functional teams
- Embedded operations engineers
- Shared on-call responsibilities

## 🔄 Organizational Change

- Start small with pilot teams
- Executive sponsorship
- Gradual process evolution

## 📊 Measuring Culture

- Deployment frequency
- Lead time for changes
- Mean time to recovery
- Employee satisfaction

## 🛠️ Practices

- Daily standups
- Retrospectives
- Blameless postmortems
- Knowledge sharing sessions

## 📝 Hands-on Exercise

- Design a team collaboration model
- Create a culture transformation plan

## ❓ Assessment

- What makes DevOps culture successful?
- How do you measure culture change?
