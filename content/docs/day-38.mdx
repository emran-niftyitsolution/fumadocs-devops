# Day 38: Change Management

## 🎯 What You'll Learn

- Why change management matters
- Change approval processes
- Change windows and rollback plans

## 🔄 Why Change Management?

Uncontrolled changes are a major source of outages. Change management reduces risk.

## 📝 Change Approval

- Peer review (pull requests)
- Automated checks (CI/CD)
- Change Advisory Board (CAB) for critical changes

## 🕒 Change Windows & Rollback

- Schedule changes during low-traffic periods
- Always have a rollback plan

## 📝 Hands-on Exercise

- Draft a change management checklist for your team

## ❓ Assessment
