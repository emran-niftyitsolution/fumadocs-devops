# Day 71: Disaster Recovery

## 🎯 What You'll Learn

- Disaster recovery planning
- Backup strategies
- Recovery time objectives (RTO)
- Business continuity

## 🚨 What is Disaster Recovery?

A plan to restore IT infrastructure and operations after a significant failure or disaster.

## 📋 DR Planning Components

- **Recovery Point Objective (RPO):** Maximum acceptable data loss
- **Recovery Time Objective (RTO):** Maximum acceptable downtime
- **Business Impact Analysis (BIA):** Identify critical systems

## 💾 Backup Strategies

- **3-2-1 Rule:** 3 copies, 2 different media, 1 offsite
- Automated backups
- Regular restore testing

## 🔄 DR Patterns

- **Cold Standby:** Backup site with no running systems
- **Warm Standby:** Backup site with minimal systems
- **Hot Standby:** Fully redundant, real-time backup

## ☁️ Cloud DR Solutions

- AWS: Cross-region replication
- Azure: Site Recovery
- Multi-cloud strategies
