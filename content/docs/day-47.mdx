# Day 47: Identity & Access Management (IAM)

## 🎯 What You'll Learn

- What is IAM?
- IAM policies and roles
- Best practices for access control

## 🧑‍💼 What is IAM?

IAM controls who can access what in your systems.

## 🛡️ IAM Policies & Roles

- **Policies:** Define permissions (JSON documents)
- **Roles:** Assign permissions to users/services
- **Groups:** Organize users

## ✅ Best Practices

- Use groups and roles, not individual permissions
- Grant least privilege
- Rotate credentials regularly

## 📝 Hands-on Exercise

- Create a new IAM role in AWS or GCP
- Attach a policy to a user or group

## ❓ Assessment

- What is the difference between a role and a policy?
- Why is least privilege important?
