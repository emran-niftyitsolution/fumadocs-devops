# Day 32: Alerting Strategies

## 🎯 What You'll Learn

- Alert fatigue and how to avoid it
- Actionable vs. noisy alerts
- Escalation policies
- On-call rotations

## 🚨 Alert Fatigue

Too many alerts can overwhelm teams. Only alert on actionable issues.

## ✅ Actionable Alerts

- Alerts should require human intervention
- Avoid alerts for self-healing or transient issues

## 📈 Escalation Policies

- Define who gets alerted and when
- Use tools like PagerDuty, Opsgenie

## 📝 Hands-on Exercise

- Review your current alerts: are any of them noisy?
- Draft an escalation policy for your team

## ❓ Assessment

- What is alert fatigue?
- Give an example of an actionable alert.
