## 📚 What You'll Learn

This documentation covers the complete DevOps engineering syllabus, organized into four phases:

- **Phase 1: Foundations** (Days 1-20) - Linux, networking, Git, scripting
- **Phase 2: CI/CD & Infrastructure as Code** (Days 21-45) - Jenkins, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform
- **Phase 3: Advanced Tools & Practices** (Days 46-70) - Monitoring, security, advanced concepts
- **Phase 4: Expert & Real-world DevOps** (Days 71-100) - SRE, scalability, portfolio building

## 📊 Learning Timeline

This curriculum is designed for **~100 days** of dedicated learning, approximately **3-4 months** of consistent study.

**Daily Commitment:** 2-4 hours per day
**Weekly Goals:** Complete one major topic per week
**Monthly Milestones:** Complete one phase per month
